Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

As people get older, there is a tendency for them to have more health coverage. However, this increases is not as great as you might expect. There is a much greater correlation between wealth and Insurance coverage.

One unsurprising find - as people make more money, they are less likely to be considered as living in poverty. However, A percentage of those sampled were in poverty despite an income over $70,000.

Smoking had some slight correlation with insurance. There is a a slight trend that as percentages of smoking rise, so do the unisnsured population.
